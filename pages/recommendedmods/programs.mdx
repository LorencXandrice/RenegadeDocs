This page will show some programs that some of our community utilize and help with your RP experience.

ShareX - Screenshot program, utilized for all kinds of RP if needed to capture things. 

Gyazo - Screenshot program, along with GIF creator. 

Lightshot - Screenshot program, pretty easy to utilize. 

OBS Studio - Program if you plan to stream / record your PC Screen.

Medal - Another screen recorder for your content, not used for streaming.

VoiceMeeter - A voice changer that works relatively well. 

Clown Fish - Another simple to use voice changer.

Please note: We do not provide support for this software listed, you can ask the community in the tech support channel in the discord and if someone knows, they will assist. It is your responsibility to know if these programs will run on your PC.
