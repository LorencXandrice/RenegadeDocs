# §3 Community Guidelines

1. Players and their characters must be 18 years or older.
2. Players must have English fluency enough to easily understand and be understood.
3. Players must have a good quality and functioning microphone and headset for roleplay.
4. Be respectful of other players in the community.\
a. Personal insults or attacks outside of roleplay are strictly prohibited.\
b. Any player caught harassing or bullying another player on the server, or outside of the server but in relation to their presence on the server, will be permanently removed and barred from the current and any future allowlist.\
c. Speaking poorly of others on stream, in chats or elsewhere will not be tolerated. 
5. Roleplay that violates the Terms of Service (ToS) or Community Guidelines of platforms including, but not limited to, <font color="#6739B7">**Twitch**</font>, <font color="#52FB18">**Kick**</font>, **TikTok**, and <font color="#FE0000">**YouTube**</font> are strictly prohibited.
6. Admin actions via <font color="#5661F5">**Discord**</font> must be given priority.\
a. If staff contacts you to discuss a rule break and you do not acknowledge the message with a response, you may be timed out until you do so.\
b. If staff contacts you to discuss a timeout, the ban will not be lifted (regardless of its initial duration) until you have spoken to the admin. 
7. Make use of the appropriate tools and channels to report issues via [`💭bug reports`](https://discord.com/channels/1083964697532973157/1185063163385872446) or request support via [`❓General Support`](https://discord.com/channels/1083964697532973157/1083965258240110594).\
a. Do *not* direct message any staff members unless it is an absolute emergency. 

If you have a concern that someone broke a community or server rule, please submit a [`🚨Player Report`](https://discord.com/channels/1083964697532973157/1083965258240110594).
